Our Story

A Legacy of
Flavour & Fire

SpiceBite was born from a simple obsession — recreating the bold, honest flavours that fill Indian homes during festivals and family gatherings. Nestled on Baner Road, Pune, we have been serving soul-warming food since 2018.

Every recipe is crafted with premium ingredients sourced locally, prepared by chefs who treat cooking as an art form. Whether it's a quiet dinner or a celebratory feast, we make every visit unforgettable.
